# Product Naming Brainstorm

Based on our core strategy—"Frictionless Evaluation," "Curriculum Alignment," and "Warm Alumni Sourcing"—here is a categorized list of potential names for the application.

## Category 1: Curriculum & Verification Focus
These names highlight our B2B University moat and the verification of actual coursework.
- **Syllabi** (or Syllabi.io)
- **CourseFit**
- **Credentia** / **Credentialed**
- **SyllabusSync**
- **UniBridge**
- **TrueSkill**

## Category 2: The "Warm Sourcing" & Alumni Focus
These names highlight the frictionless pipeline and the connection to alumni networks.
- **WarmHire**
- **AlumniTrack**
- **VettedFlow**
- **HireLoop**
- **SignalMatch** (Contrasting with the "Noise" of LinkedIn)
- **The Warm Feed**

## Category 3: Alignment & Evaluation Focus
These names highlight the "Intelligence Layer" and the prescriptive gap analysis.
- **TrueFit**
- **SkillMap**
- **GapAlign**
- **Contextual**
- **Align.io**
- **MapMyCourse**

## Category 4: Abstract / Modern Tech
Short, memorable names that sound like a premium SaaS product.
- **Lumina** (Shedding light on the "black box" of career outcomes)
- **Adept**
- **Pathos**
- **Nexus** (Connecting students, university, and employer)
- **Aura**
