# LinkedIn / warm outreach (templates)

Use after you have a real reason to connect (applied, referral, shared interest). No mass spam.

## Connection note (short)

Hi {{NAME}} — I applied for the {{ROLE}} role at {{COMPANY}}. {{ONE_SPECIFIC_SENTENCE e.g. admired product X / your post on Y}}. Would welcome connecting.

## Follow-up after apply (recruiter)

Hi {{NAME}}, I submitted my application for {{ROLE}} ({{REQ_ID if any}}). {{ONE_LINE_WHY_FIT}}. Happy to share a one-pager or take a brief screen if helpful.
