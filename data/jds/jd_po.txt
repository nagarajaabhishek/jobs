Job Title: Product Owner

About the Role:
We are seeking a certified Product Owner to drive agile product delivery and own the product backlog for a fast-growing SaaS platform. You will act as the bridge between business stakeholders and engineering teams, ensuring the team always has a groomed, high-priority backlog ready for execution.

Responsibilities:
- Own and manage the product backlog, writing high-quality user stories, epics, and acceptance criteria.
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives as a servant leader.
- Prioritize features using RICE, MoSCoW, and WSJF frameworks aligned with business goals.
- Collaborate with stakeholders to gather requirements, define product vision, and communicate the roadmap.
- Conduct user research, A/B testing, and customer interviews to validate product decisions.
- Enforce Definition of Ready (DoR) and Definition of Done (DoD) to ensure quality delivery.
- Track and report sprint velocity, burndown charts, and team KPIs through Jira dashboards.
- Manage UAT sessions and coordinate sign-offs between QA, Dev, and business teams.
- Leverage AI tools and automation to streamline product discovery and backlog refinement.
- Define GTM strategy for product launches and align internal teams on release plans.
- Drive product-led growth (PLG) initiatives and monitor NPS, funnel metrics, and cohort analysis.

Requirements:
- Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) or equivalent certification required.
- 2+ years of experience as a Product Owner or Product Manager in an Agile environment.
- Strong proficiency in Jira, Confluence, Linear, and backlog management tools.
- Experience with user story mapping, sprint planning, and backlog grooming.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP), REST APIs, and SDLC processes.
- Proficiency with analytics tools: Power BI, Amplitude, Google Analytics.
- Experience with AI/ML product strategy, LLMs, prompt engineering, and agentic workflows.
- Strong stakeholder communication, conflict resolution, and servant leadership skills.
- Knowledge of Lean, Kanban, SAFe frameworks.
- Experience with PRDs, BRDs, and functional specifications.
- Background in Computer Science, Engineering Management, or related field preferred.
