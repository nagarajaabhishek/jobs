Job Title: Program Manager / Project Manager

About the Role:
We are looking for a Program Manager to lead complex, cross-functional programs from ideation to delivery. You will drive operational efficiency, manage stakeholder relationships, and ensure consistent execution across multiple concurrent workstreams.

Responsibilities:
- Own end-to-end program lifecycle management: scope definition, planning, execution, monitoring, and closure.
- Develop and maintain detailed project plans, roadmaps, risk registers, and status reports.
- Lead cross-functional teams across engineering, product, marketing, and operations to deliver on program objectives.
- Identify critical path dependencies, mitigate schedule variance risks, and drive issue resolution.
- Define and track KPIs, OKRs, and program health metrics using Power BI and Google Analytics dashboards.
- Architect and deploy SOPs, process documentation, and operational playbooks to improve team efficiency.
- Manage stakeholder communication, facilitate executive briefings, and present data-driven insights to leadership.
- Drive business development, partnership negotiations, and sponsorship proposals to secure program funding.
- Apply Agile and Lean methodologies to improve sprint velocity and remove systemic process bottlenecks.
- Conduct retrospectives, root cause analysis, and implement continuous improvement initiatives.
- Coordinate UAT, feature releases, and change management across technical and business teams.
- Support GTM strategy execution including launch planning, vendor management, and go-live coordination.

Requirements:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Engineering Management, Computer Science, Business, or related field.
- 2+ years of experience in program management, project management, or operations.
- Proficiency in Agile, Scrum, Waterfall, Kanban, and SAFe methodologies.
- Hands-on experience with Jira, Asana, Notion, Microsoft Project, and project tracking tools.
- Strong skills in risk management, stakeholder communication, and cross-functional leadership.
- Experience with Power BI, Google Analytics, Excel, and data-driven decision making.
- Familiarity with Python, SQL, REST APIs, and technical program delivery.
- Experience with SOP design, process improvement, and operational efficiency programs.
- Strong business development, partnership, and sponsorship management experience.
- PMP, CAPM, or Google Project Management certification is a plus.
- Lean Six Sigma Green Belt (LSSGB) or equivalent is highly valued.
