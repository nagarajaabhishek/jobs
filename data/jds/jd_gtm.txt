Job Title: Go-To-Market (GTM) Manager / Technical Product Marketing Manager

About the Role:
We are looking for a GTM Manager to drive product launches, community-led growth, and developer adoption for our AI-powered platform. You will own the end-to-end go-to-market strategy, bridging the gap between engineering innovation and commercial adoption.

Responsibilities:
- Define and execute go-to-market (GTM) strategy for new product launches across B2B and B2C segments.
- Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and TAM/SAM/SOM modeling to identify growth opportunities.
- Build and execute multi-channel user acquisition campaigns including community-led growth, content, and partnerships.
- Collaborate with engineering and product teams to translate technical features into compelling value propositions.
- Develop product positioning, messaging frameworks, and technical whitepapers for target personas.
- Drive community building and developer relations (DevRel) initiatives to grow platform adoption.
- Own NPS tracking, funnel optimization, A/B testing, and conversion rate improvement programs.
- Manage strategic partnerships, sponsorship negotiations, and co-marketing agreements.
- Produce content strategy including blogs, case studies, podcasts, and event programming.
- Analyze user behavior using Google Analytics, Amplitude, and Power BI to inform GTM iterations.
- Execute sales enablement by developing competitive battlecards, demo scripts, and onboarding collateral.
- Coordinate launch logistics including event management, vendor coordination, and stakeholder alignment.

Requirements:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Marketing, Business, or related field.
- 2+ years of experience in product marketing, growth, GTM, or developer relations.
- Strong proficiency in GTM strategy, product positioning, and market research.
- Experience with community-led growth, influencer partnerships, and content marketing.
- Familiarity with Python, SQL, n8n, Zapier, and workflow automation tools.
- Proficiency with Google Analytics, Amplitude, Power BI, and A/B testing frameworks.
- Experience with Figma, Notion, and marketing operations tools.
- Strong technical writing, product storytelling, and stakeholder communication skills.
- Experience running events, podcasts, and community programs at scale.
- Background in AI/ML products, developer platforms, or SaaS GTM is highly preferred.
- Lean Six Sigma or Google Project Management certification is a plus.
