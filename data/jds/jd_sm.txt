Job Title: Scrum Master

About the Role:
We are hiring an entry-level to associate Scrum Master to facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach teams toward high-performance delivery. You will serve as a servant leader committed to continuous improvement and team velocity.

Responsibilities:
- Facilitate all Scrum ceremonies: sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives.
- Remove impediments and protect the development team from external distractions and scope creep.
- Coach team members on Agile values, Scrum principles, and continuous improvement practices.
- Maintain and update the Jira sprint board, ensuring 100% visibility of work in progress.
- Track and report sprint velocity, burndown charts, and team capacity metrics to stakeholders.
- Support the Product Owner in backlog refinement, writing acceptance criteria, and user story mapping.
- Facilitate conflict resolution and foster a culture of psychological safety and collaboration.
- Identify and implement process improvements through retrospective-driven action items.
- Apply WSJF and MoSCoW prioritization frameworks to help the team maximize value delivery.
- Leverage AI tools and generative AI for automated documentation and backlog management.
- Build and maintain stakeholder relationships through transparent communication and reporting.
- Drive Definition of Ready (DoR) and Definition of Done (DoD) adoption across the team.

Requirements:
- Certified Scrum Master (CSM) or Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) required.
- 1-2 years of experience as a Scrum Master, Agile Coach, or delivery analyst.
- Strong knowledge of Scrum, Kanban, SAFe, and Lean frameworks.
- Hands-on experience with Jira, Linear, Confluence, Miro, and collaboration tools.
- Excellent facilitation, servant leadership, and impediment removal skills.
- Experience with burndown charts, velocity tracking, and sprint metrics.
- Familiarity with SDLC, CI/CD, and software development processes.
- Strong communication, stakeholder management, and conflict resolution skills.
- Experience using AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini) for documentation and sprint support.
- Background in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field preferred.
- Google Project Management or Agile certification is a plus.
